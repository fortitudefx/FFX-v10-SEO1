What's covered

Four weeks. One complete framework built from the ground up.

Broad enough to give you the picture. Specific enough to know exactly what you are walking into.

Week 01

Foundation & Structure

We start by cleaning up how you see the market. Structure, directional bias, premium and discount, multi-timeframe alignment. Everything that must be in place before execution is ever relevant.

Week 02

Liquidity & Candle Theory

No candle is random. No timeframe is noise. This week builds the ability to read every candle as information — strong versus weak liquidity, which zones actually matter, and why institutions leave the evidence they do.

Week 03

The 5 Mechanical Entry Models

This is where the framework becomes a trigger. 5 mechanical entry models applied to live market conditions. CHOCH defined without grey zones. Either the model is printing — or it is not. No interpretation required.

Week 04

Refinement & Pattern Clarity

The goal at this stage is not more information — it is better recognition. Review, repetition, and sharpening the decisions that separate consistent execution from consistent hesitation.
