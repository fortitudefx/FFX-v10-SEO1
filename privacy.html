Privacy Policy

FortitudeFX  ·  Last updated: April 2026  ·  Applies to all FortitudeFX web properties

1. Who we are

FortitudeFX is a trading education brand operated from the United Arab Emirates. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website or sign up for our waitlist.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@fortitudefx.com.

2. What data we collect

When you submit the FortitudeFX waitlist form, we collect the following information:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Your selected path (VIP Discord, Bootcamp, or Stay Connected)
  • Your selected membership plan and price point (if applicable)
  • Your self-reported trading stage (beginner, intermediate, or advanced)
  • Any optional note you choose to include
  • The promotional offer active at the time of your submission

We do not collect payment information through our waitlist form. No card details are entered or stored at this stage.

3. How we use your data

We process your personal data based on your consent when you submit the waitlist form. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us or using the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To reserve your place on the FortitudeFX waitlist
  • To send you relevant updates about your selected path
  • To notify you before any payment is requested
  • To personalise communications using your name and selected preferences

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Where your data is stored

Your contact information is stored securely in Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), our email marketing platform. Brevo is GDPR-compliant and stores data on EU-based infrastructure. You can review Brevo's own privacy policy at brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy.

5. No payment today

Submitting the waitlist form does not constitute a purchase or payment agreement. Your card will not be charged or reserved at any point during the waitlist process. You will receive an email notification before any payment is requested, giving you the opportunity to confirm, change your plan, or opt out entirely.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Unsubscribe from communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly.

7. Cookies and tracking

Our website does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party analytics beyond what is necessary to operate the site. If this changes, this policy will be updated and you will be informed.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, please reach out:

support@fortitudefx.com
