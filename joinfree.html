The Framework

Catch The Wick — explained simply.

You don’t need the full strategy to understand the concept. Two candles tell a complete story. Here’s what you’re actually learning to read.

1

Candle 1 establishes direction

The first candle creates a reference point — a high, a low, a liquidity pool sitting above or below price. The market has told you where it needs to go.

2

Candle 2 sweeps liquidity

Price extends beyond that reference point — sweeping the liquidity sitting at the highs or lows. The wick is the footprint. That sweep is the trigger.

3

Lower timeframe confirms execution

Drop to the lower timeframe. Wait for the structural confirmation. Enter with defined risk. The framework handles the rest.

The goal is not prediction. The goal is structured participation — after liquidity is taken, after the market has shown its hand, after the story is confirmed. That is Catch The Wick.
