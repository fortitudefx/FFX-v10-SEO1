CTW™ Entry Mechanism

The Framework

Four core models.
Everything else is a variation.

EM1

Liquidation Entry

A strong level is swept. Immediate orderflow change. You enter on the wick of the liquidation candle — before the continuation move begins.

"You're in before the move."

EM2

Zone Reaction Entry

No liquidation. Price mitigates a valid supply or demand zone. The reaction candle forms with OFC. The zone holds. You follow the institutional hand.

"Zone confirms. You follow."

EM3

Breakout Entry

Shallow pullback into a valid zone. Momentum locked in. Breakout entry on the continuation. You ride the session with the trend confirmed.

"Momentum locked. You ride it."

EM4

Failed Reaction

Supply and demand collide at the zone. The first reaction fails — and the failure reveals the stronger hand. You take the side the market just proved.

"Let them fight. You back the winner."
